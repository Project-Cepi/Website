About Cepi

The MMORPG takes place in a vast fantasy world filled with magic and never before seen wonders.

Where you can venture with friends and face challenges, discover unique regions, and become a part of the immersive world.

Cepi's development is meant to be:

  • Open Source (anyone and everyone can contribute with the right skills)
  • Modifiable (make your own bosses, mobs, and experiences yourself!)
  • Exploratory (find new places and delve into new content to satisfy any power trip!)
Come join our beta server! (cepi.world)
